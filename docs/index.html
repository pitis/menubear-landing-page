What we provide

MenuBear is a web-based software that allows restaurants to organize their menus, customize them in a few clicks and offer a unique experience when ordering. MenuBear provides a wide range of functionalities for restaurant owners to take control of their menus, communicate with clients and increase sales.

Easier to Manage your Business

  • Real-time customer analytics

Our dashboard provides real-time insights on customers’ buying history and behaviors, which can, in turn, allow businesses to achieve higher retention rates and increased revenue. Such insights allow the marketing and sales team to concentrate on the right customers at the right time.

  • Better forecasting

When a businesses have more insights into the buying cycle of customers, future demands can be better predicted. Our dashboard can be used to plan more effectively for demand fluctuations in business cycles.

  • Easy to use

Whether it's inserting more products or verifying your best sellers, the dashboard is easy to use and very user-friendly.
